Welcome to Game marathon.

Goal: develop one game a day for 21 days, in 2 hours each.

Result: marathon lasted for 7 days from 2013-11-14 to 2013-11-20.

Game list:

  1. Tick-Tack-Toe
    • What could be simplier?
  2. 15 puzzle
    • 4 images, difficulty increases: 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, etc.
    • Known issue: parity bug, in 50% of shuffles puzzle cannot be solved. I could fix it, but.. Naaah..
  3. Tetris
    • Classic
  4. Minesweeper
    • Tip: Click/right-click on numbers to uncover/flag 3x3 area.
  5. Pong!
    • Some action introduced. Also, with sound.
  6. Collapse
    • Relaxing. I got wisdom tooth removed that day.
    • Music: "Pamgaea" Kevin MacLeod (incompetech.com)
  7. Match-3
    • Time and effort constraint really kicks in, so...
    • With sound.
    • Music: "Killing Time" Kevin MacLeod (incompetech.com)
